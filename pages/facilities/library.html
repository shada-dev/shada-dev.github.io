Digital Library

Our College Library constitutes about 15,000 volumes of books including text books and references of various engineering and supporting departments. In addition, it comprises 230 educational CDs; serves as a true repository of knowledge to the engineering graduates. Our vision is to build a multifaceted students society to tackle the challenges in the real world.

Instructions

  1. Books will be issued only on production of valid ID card.
  2. Books borrowed must be returned on or before the due date.
  3. Renewal of the books cannot be more than one time.
  4. Books have to be physically presented for renewals.
  5. Books can be renewed if there are no reservations.
  6. If the books are missed by the user, he/she will replace the books at any cost.
  7. Users are requested to verify the physical condition of the book/s before borrowing.
  8. Users are requested to place their bags/belongings outside the library.
  9. Users are requested to Maintain absolute Silence in the library.
