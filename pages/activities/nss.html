About NSS

National service scheme popularly called as NSS is an Indian Government-Sponsored, public service program conducted by the Department of Youth Affairs and sports of the Government of India. NSS was started on the Gandhiji’s Centenary year, 1969. Aimed to improve the student’s personality towards community service. NSS is a voluntary of student’s at Colleges, Universities and at +2 Grade level integrating the Campus & Community. The Cardinal principle of NSS is that Student’s themselves involve in organising the programme, Teachers and students work together in the task of nation building.

Motto

Major motto of UCETK in NSS is that “Not me but you”, which offers the students with self-less behaviour. Explaining that the happiness of the individual lies only with the development of the society .UCETK has proved it by conducting many activities
